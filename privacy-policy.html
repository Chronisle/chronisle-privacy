Privacy Policy for Chronisle

Last updated: 07 December 2025

Chronisle (“the App”) is developed and provided by the individual developer Achillefs Kalathas (“we”, “us”, “our”). We take your privacy seriously. Chronisle is designed with privacy first, and we collect no personal data of any kind.

1. Information We Collect

Chronisle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

  • The App does not require an account
  • Does not connect to external servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking technologies
  • Does not access contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or location
  • Does not send any data outside your device
  • All app data (focus sessions, log entries, color selections, settings) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it

2. How We Use Your Data

Since the App collects no data, there is no processing or usage of personal information of any kind.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party services, SDKs, or tracking tools. Chronisle does not share any information with third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

Chronisle is suitable for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, the App complies with privacy protections for children.

5. Security

All app functionality operates directly on your device. No data is transmitted over the internet.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
